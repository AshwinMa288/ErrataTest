  raster-scanned LL3 coefficients are&#8203;</p>
  <p>&#8203;</p>
  <p>[64, 32, 42, 54, 50, 60, 40, 70]&#8203;</p>

  <p>Then, after differential encoding, they would get

  <p>&#8203;</p>
  <p>&#8203;</p>

  <p>...&#8203;</p>
  <p>The coefficients from LL3 also undergo differential
